Goal

The aim of the Snake Game is to increase the snake length as much as possible. In order to make it grow, the player needs to guide the snake and eat berries. But, the snake cannot eat itself. How long will your snake be? Lets play and find it out!


How to play

The game controls are straightforward. The snake will start running from the top-left corner horizontally. The buttons situated in the right side will allow the user to change the snake dirrection
